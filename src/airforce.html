Indian Air Force

We live by chance, we love by choice, we kill by profession.

The Indian Air Force is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces. Its complement of personnel and aircraft assets ranks third amongst the air forces of the world. Its primary mission is to secure Indian airspace and to conduct aerial warfare during armed conflict.

Headquarters: New Delhi
Founded: 8 October 1932, India
Role: Aerial warfare
Size: 170,576 active personnel; 140,000 reserve personnel; Approx 1926+ aircraft
Helicopter: CH-47 Chinook, Dhruv, Chetak, Cheetah, Mi-8, Mi-17, Mi-26
Trainer: Hawk Mk 132, HJT-16 Kiran, Pilatus C-7 Mk II
Fighters: SEPECAT Jaguar, Dassault Rafale, Sukhoi Su-30MKI.
