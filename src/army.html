Indian Army

We live by chance, we love by choice, we kill by profession.

The Indian Army is the land-based branch and the largest component of the Indian Armed Forces. The President of India is the Supreme Commander of the Indian Army, and its professional head is the Chief of Army Staff, who is a four-star general.
Headquarters: New Delhi
Founded: 1 April 1895, India
Motto(s): सेवा परमो धर्म: (Sanskrit); Sevā Paramo Dharmaḥ (ISO); transl. "Service Before Self"
Size: 1,237,117 active personnel; 960,000 reserve personnel; ~296 manned aircraft
Anniversaries Army Day: 15 January
