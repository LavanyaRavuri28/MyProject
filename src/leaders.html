Chief of the Army Staff

General Manoj Pande, PVSM, AVSM, VSM, ADC

(30 Apr 2022 to Till Date)

1. General Manoj Pande, PVSM, AVSM, VSM, ADC an alumnus of the National Defence Academy, was commissioned in December 1982 in the Corps of Engineers (The Bombay Sappers). The General Officer has commanded an Engineer Regiment during Operation PARAKRAM in the sensitive Pallanwala Sector of Jammu and Kashmir, along the Line of Control. The General Officer is a graduate of Staff College, Camberley (United Kingdom) and attended the Higher Command (HC) and National Defence College (NDC) Courses.

2. In his four decades of distinguished military career, he has tenanted important and challenging command and staff appointments in different operational environments which include command of an Engineer Brigade in the Western Theatre, as part of Strike Corps, an Infantry Brigade along Line of Control in Jammu and Kashmir, a Mountain Division in the High Altitude Area of Western Ladakh and command of a Corps, deployed along the Line of Actual Control (LAC) and in Counter Insurgency Operations area of Eastern Command.  His staff exposures include Brigade Major of a Mountain Brigade in the North East, Assistant Military Secretary (AMS) in Military Secretary’s Branch, Colonel Q of a Mountain Division in High Altitude Area and Brigadier General Staff (Operations) at Headquarters Eastern Command. The General Officer has served as Chief Engineer in the United Nations Mission in Ethiopia and Eritrea. He has also tenanted the appointments of Additional Director General in the Military Operations Directorate at Army Headquarters, Chief of Staff Headquarters Southern Command and Director General Discipline Ceremonial & Welfare at the Army Headquarters. He was Commander-in-Chief Andaman & Nicobar Command (CINCAN) from June 2020 to May 2021 and General Officer Commanding-in-Chief Eastern Command from Jun 2021 to Jan 2022.

3. He held the appointment of Vice Chief of Army Staff before assuming the appointment of the Chief of the Army Staff on 30 Apr 2022.

Vice Chief of the Army Staff

Lt Gen Baggavalli
Somashekar Raju
UYSM, AVSM, YSM

Lt Gen Baggavalli Somashekar Raju, UYSM, AVSM, YSM, an alumnus of Sainik School Bijapur and National Defence Academy was commissioned in the 11th Battalion The Jat Regiment on 15 Dec 1984. He later commanded his Battalion during OP PARAKRAM in Western theatre and in Jammu & Kashmir. He also holds the distinction of commanding the Uri Brigade along the Line of Control, Counter Insurgency Force and Chinar Corps in Kashmir Valley. The General Officer was also Commandant, Indian Military Training Team in Bhutan.

The officer during his illustrious career spanning 38 years, has tenanted important regimental, staff and instructional appointments in the Army HQ and field formations, to include Brigade Major in an Infantry Brigade, Colonel MS Legal in IHQ of MoD (Army), Brigadier General Staff of operationally active Corps on the western front, DDG Military Operations & DGSD in IHQ of MoD (Army). Prior to taking over as Vice Chief of Army Staff the General Officer was tenating the appointment of Director General of Military Operation at IHQ of MoD (Army). The General officer is also the Colonel of the JAT Regiment.

The officer has had instructional tenures at Defence Service Staff College, Wellington and Higher Command Wing, Army War College, Mhow. The officer is also a qualified helicopter pilot, having done operational flying in Somalia, as part of UNOSOM II. The General Officer has attended all important career courses in India and has the privilege of doing his NDC at Royal College of Defence Studies, UK. He has also done his distinguished Masters programme in Counter Terrorism, at Naval Postgraduate School, Monterey, USA. The General Officer has taken over as Vice Chief of Army Staff on 01 May 2022.

Army Commanders

Lieutenant General Ajai Kumar Singh

Lt Gen Rana Pratap Kalita

Lt Gen Yogendra Dimri

Lieutenant General Ajai Kumar Singh, AVSM, YSM, SM, VSM, an alumnus of St Gabriel’s Academy Roorkee, National Defence Academy and Indian Military Academy was commissioned into the Seventh Battalion of the Eleventh Gorkha Rifles in December 1984. The General officer is a graduate of Defence Services Staff College and has attended prestigious Higher Command and National Defence College courses.
In his 38 years of distinguished military career, he has the distinction of tenating challenging command and staff appointments in varied terrain. He has vast experience in Counter Insurgency, High Altitude, Glaciated and Desert operations.

Lt Gen Rana Pratap Kalita, UYSM, AVSM, SM, VSM and alumnus of Sainik School Goalpara, National Defence Academy and Indian Military Academy. He was commissioned in 9 KUMAON on 09 June 1984. The General Officer is a graduate of Defence Service Staff College and has attended Higher Command and National Defence College courses.
In his 37 years of distinguished military career, he has distinction of tenating important and challenging command and staff appointments in North East and Jammu and Kashmir.

Lt Gen Yogendra Dimri was commissioned into the Corps of Engineers (The Bombay Sappers) on 17 December 1983. An alumnus of National Defence Academy, President’s Gold Medal awardee at Indian Military Academy being first in Order of Merit, ‘Silver Grenade’ of his Young Officers’ Course and Gold Medal awardee in the Engineers Degree Course, the General Officer has a distinguished and illustrious military career spanning more than 37 years. He went on to attend prestigious Army courses at Defence Services Staff College, Defence Services Command & Staff College (Dhaka), Army War College and National Defence College.

Deputy Chief of Army Staff

Lt Gen Shantanu Dayal,
UYSM, AVSM, SM, VSM,
DCOAS (CD&S)

Lt Gen MV Suchindra Kumar,
AVSM, YSM**, VSM,
DCOAS (Strat)

Lieutenant General Shantanu Dayal is an Infantry Officer commissioned in Garhwal Rifles in 1984. He is an alumnus of National Defence Academy,Pune Indian Military Academy, Dehradun, Defence Service Staff College, Ooty, College of Defence Management, Secunderabad and National Defence College, Delhi.

Lieutenant General Shantanu Dayal took part in active operations in Sri Lanka, Jammu & Kashmir and North Eastern States. The General Officer Commanded a Garhwal Rifles unit in Jammu & Kashmir, a Mountain Brigade in North East States, an Infantry Division in Jammu & Kashmir and a Corps in North Eastern States. He has also served in Rashtriya Rifles as well as in Iraq and Kuwait on United Nations Mission. He has been an Instructor at Commando Wing, GSO 3 in an Infantry Brigade, GSO 2 and GSO 1 in Military Operations Directorate at Army Headquarters, Col GS in an Infantry Division, Deputy Commander in a Mountain Brigade, BGS (Operations) in a Command Headquarters, DDG (D&V), ADG (Proc) and DG DC&W at Integrated Headquarters of Ministry of Defence (Army).

Lieutenant General MV Suchindra Kumar, Ati Vishisht Seva Medal, Yudh Seva Medal**, Vishisht Seva Medal assumed the appointment of Deputy Chief of Army Staff (Strategy), Integrated Headquarters of Ministry of Defence (Army) on 01 July 2022.

The General Officer is an alumnus of National Defence Academy, Khadakwasla. He was commissioned into 1 ASSAM REGIMENT in June 1985. He commanded 59 Rashtriya Rifles Battalion (ASSAM), an Infantry Brigade and an Infantry Division on the Line of Control. He has also commanded the highly active White Knight Corps. The Gen offr had the privilege of assuming the mantle of the Col of the Regt of Assam Regt and Arunachal Scouts on 01 Mar 2021.
